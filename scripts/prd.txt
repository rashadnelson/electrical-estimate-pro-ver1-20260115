## ⚡ Electrical Estimate Pro: Masterplan

### Vision
Electrical Estimate Pro helps electrical professionals send quotes that are accurate, professional, and fast. Create professional electrical estimates in under a minute. No more messy paperwork or lost quotes—just sharp, branded PDFs your clients will trust.

### Mission
Become the go-to estimate tool for electrical contractors who value speed, accuracy, and professionalism.

---

### Problem Statement
Electrical contractors often rely on gut feel, scribbled notes, or messy spreadsheets to price jobs—risking undercharging, losing money on materials, or appearing unprofessional to customers.

### Goal
Empower electrical professionals with a pricing assistant that's accurate, fast, and trustworthy—like a digital estimate sheet that never lets you leave money on the table.

---

### Target Audience

**Primary Users:**
- Independent electricians and small electrical companies (≤15 employees)
- Residential and commercial electrical contractors
- Primarily male, aged 25–55
- Mix of tech comfort: some use smartphones daily; others prefer paper

**User Needs:**
- Speed: Create an estimate on-site in under 60 seconds
- Accuracy: Never forget to include permits, wire costs, or code compliance fees
- Professionalism: Send a branded PDF that builds client trust
- Simplicity: No steep learning curve

---

### Core Features (MVP)

#### 1. Fast Estimate Builder
- Select job type (panel upgrade, outlet install, rewire, etc.)
- Add line items (labor hours, materials, fixtures, permits)
- Auto-calculate totals with tax option
- Add optional notes (scope, conditions, timeline)

#### 2. PDF Export
- One-click professional PDF generation
- Clean, branded layout with company info and license number
- Auto-includes: Date, estimate number, itemized breakdown, totals, payment terms
- Optional: Terms & conditions footer

#### 3. License Key System
- Format: ELEC-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX
- Validates subscription tier and access level

#### 4. Template System
- Save frequently used estimates as templates
- Quick-load for common electrical jobs

#### 5. Client Management (Basic)
- Store client name, address, phone, email
- Auto-fill for repeat customers

### Technical Stack
- **Frontend:** React + Vite + TypeScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Hono.js
- **Database:** Neon PostgreSQL with Drizzle ORM
- **Auth:** Better-Auth
- **Payments:** Stripe
- **PDF:** React-PDF
- **Hosting:** Netlify
